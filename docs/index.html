About Me

I'm a Computer Science Engineer at Machine Learning Reply, in Lingotto, Turin. Previously, I graduated with my BS and MS from Politecnico di Milano. My experimental activity are based in Area42 Lab concerns two topics: the integration of machine learning models to allow robotic agents to navigate environments and manipulate the objects present in it and the integration of machine learning models to allow digital humans to interact with users by capturing emotions and transmitting them.

The robotic agents I use are Boston Dynamics SPOT with ARM, Jackal, Reeman. These agents are used in a collaborative scenario for object inspection and manipulation tasks in environments both known and unknown to the agents.

The topic relating to the use of Digital Humans on an Unreal Engine basis concerns the integration of Machine learning models for the study of the research area relating to Affective Computing and therefore processing the user's emotions during a conversation and then allowing digital human to synthesize and express them.

In Reply i'm member of AI Community of Practice Group that is committed to pioneering the application of Artificial General Intelligence, focusing on Large Language Model Agents, Multi-Modality and the latest research directions like Objective-Driven Ai, to bridge the gap between advanced research and real-world scenarios.

Project Page Paper

Implementation of the most recent advances in embodied AI from Google and Meta on Boston Dynamics Spot Robot. The ultimate goal of the work is to develop a long-horizon planning coordinator who coordinates Spot robot to complete long-horizon tasks based on their short-horizon skills. The context will be that of navigation and mobile manipulation, i.e., the robots will be asked to fetch objects and move them to other points in a pre-set area. All this will pass through the short-term skills built-in in the Spot SDK, Meta's recent model for artificial visual cortex (VC-1), and Google's vision-language (VLM) model for visual-language navigation for give long-horizon tasks to robots through natural language prompts in the area.
